Our Purpose

WE HELP THOUSANDS OF CHILDREN TO GET THEIR EDUCATION, NOW WE NEED YOUR HELP.

learn more

volunteer

Do you belief that everyone has the right to access good quality education?

We believe that when communities build communities, we create stronger bonds, provide a sense of belonging and create safer and thriving communities the world over.

Our Approach to Solving illiteracy in the Nuba Mountains is by; Working with Both Private and public schools to convert school libraries into community libraries for the benefit of all members of the community during prescribed hours.

In our team nothing goes to waste. We use all donated books & funds to eradicate illiteracy and poverty in the Nuba Mountains and surrounding Regions and Africa as a whole. When the books are shipped a small team in Nuba awaits for the arrival of the donated books to set up new and stock existing libraries in all parts of the Nuba mountains.
