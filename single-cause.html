WITH YOUR SUPPORT WE CAN GET 20,000 BOOKS TO THE NUBA MOUNTAINS

Right now our biggest priority is to ship 20 thousands books that has been donated to us to the Nuba Mountains. We want to educate and bring change to the people in Nuba with one book at a time and we believe that it can start with you. With your support we would be to the books into the hands of the children in the Nuba Mountain.

“Education is the backbone of the economy, it is the single best guarantor of a better standard of living and eradicating poverty.”

Support us with shipping 20K+ books to the Nuba Mountains.

Funds are vital for creating a fully functional and thriving ecosystem. Primarily the funds will be used to;

  • Transport 20K+ books to the Nuba mountains.
  • Pay for transportation costs to get the books to school libraries in the Nuba Mountains.
  • Pay library staff and volunteers nominal wages to manage and maintain the library.
  • Build libraries and fund programs that enhance the learning experience of the students.

One in Four believes that when communities build communities, we create stronger bonds, provide a sense of belonging and create safer and thriving communities the world over.

Our goal is to eradicate illiteracy and poverty in the Nuba Mountains and surrounding Regions and Africa as a whole. One in Four will use donated books to Set up new or stock existing libraries in all counties in the Nuba mountains to;

  • Improve community literacy standards through volunteer led reading and writing classes and workshops.
  • Reduce student teacher ratio and promote equitable access to learning resources removing distance to education and other home constraints.
  • Provide tailored learning programs to students and combine information resources and technologies.
  • Equip students with the literacy and computer skills necessary to succeed in a dynamic technological, social and economic environment.
